Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How often should I water my plants?

Watering frequency depends on the plant species, pot size, and environmental conditions. Generally, most indoor plants need watering when the top 1-2 inches of soil feels dry.

Do you offer plant care advice?

Yes! We provide detailed care instructions with every purchase and our expert team is always available for consultation.

What is your shipping policy?

We ship nationwide with carefully packaged plants to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. Orders typically arrive within 3-5 business days.

Do you offer returns?

We have a 30-day return policy for plants that arrive damaged or unhealthy. Contact our customer service team within 24 hours of delivery for assistance.

Are your plants pet-friendly?

We clearly label all our plants as pet-friendly or not. Look for the "Pet Safe" badge on product pages, or ask our team for recommendations.
